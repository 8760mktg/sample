Carlos De la Torre

Top-notch Real Estate Agent in Puerto Vallarta Region

Carlos is a Mexican National born in Queretaro. A holder of both a Bachelor of Business Administration degree from the University of Texas at Pan Am (Rio Grande Valley), and a law degree from the University of Guadalajara, Carlos has been living in the Puerto Vallarta area since the 1980’s, Upon arrival in the bay, Carlos spent a decade in the marketing industry, first in resort ownership marketing, then with Johnson & Johnson and lastly with America Economía magazine -a subsidiary of the Dow Jones Group, the publishers of the Wall Street Journal. In addition to his marketing experience Carlos was Director of Puerto Vallarta’s first university accredited Spanish language school in Puerto Vallarta,- CEPE - part of the University of Guadalajara. Since becoming a real estate agent seventeen years ago, Carlos has passed all Federal Certifications available in the field and he has been an active member of the national realtors’ association in Mexico, better known as AMPI. Particularly experienced in beachfront developments from Mismaloya to Punta Mita.
Fluent in Spanish, English and Chinese.
